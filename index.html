Raising Cane's Market Entry

For my Global Branding class, I created a market entry plan for Raising Cane’s expansion into the Netherlands. I analyzed cultural insights, consumer behaviors, and local competition to understand how American fast-food brands are perceived abroad. The project emphasized Dutch preferences for value-driven meals, halal options, and sustainable practices, leading to recommendations on how Cane’s could adapt its branding and menu to stand out in a competitive market.

Liquid Death Research Project

For my research class, I worked with a team to analyze Liquid Death’s marketing strategy and consumer perceptions through 12 in-depth interviews. Findings revealed strong appeal in the brand’s recyclable aluminum packaging and bold identity. Applied models such as SMCRE, AIDA, and SWOT to assess positioning among Gen Z, millennials, and health-conscious consumers, ultimately providing strategic recommendations for brand growth.
